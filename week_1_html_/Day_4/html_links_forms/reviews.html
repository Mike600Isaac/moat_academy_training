What is software engineering

Software engineering is a branch of computer science focused on the systematic design, development, testing, and maintenance of software applications. It applies engineering principles to the creation of software, aiming for quality, reliability, and maintainability. This involves a disciplined approach, encompassing requirements analysis, design, coding, testing, and ongoing maintenance.
